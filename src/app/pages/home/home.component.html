Welcome to the
Digial Donut Shop!

In the heart of the largest city in the metaverse, tucked between skyscrapers, stands the Digital Donut Shop.

It’s an old donut bakery, transformed to a cultural landmark for those at the cutting edge of art and technology. Artists and Degens loiter around the parking lot under flickering neon lights, coming in and out of the imposing metal door at the front of the shop. As they pass a sign labeled “Holders Only”, some display a digital token to the scanner, while others turn a shining key in the lock. The scanner beeps, the hinges creak, and the members enter the transmuted shop within.
