The world is currently experiencing a renaissance of art and technology, and Digital Donuts aims to be a central gathering place for those building and guiding this future. Digital Donut NFT’s are your access into this community.

Why The Digital Donut Shop?

In 3D art, the donut is often the first piece an artist will make. The first step of a great journey, sparking that realization of what is and can be possible. The same inspiration is happening in NFT’s, as digital architects build this future into Web 3.0. The donut is also a universally loved snack, the type you buy a dozen of and share with family and friends as you gather and discuss ideas. Our shop is a place for 3D artists of all skill levels to connect with a passionate community, whom in turn will be able to gather and collect great pieces from those just starting to shape the next era of art.

Core Principles

Moving forward we aim to build a community that guides itself based on these core principles.

Build value in holding Digital Donut NFT’s

Support and foster growth of 3D artists

Make large and consistent donations to food banks local to our community

Introduce more people to NFT’s and expand what is possible with verifiable ownership via NFT’s

Roadmap

Opening Up Shop

Phase 2 Website and Discord launch, core artists are announced. Whitelist is finalized and mint passes are claimed

The First Batch

Original Batch of Digital Donuts are Minted via website

Key is Key

All wallets that minted will be given one week to mint a free key to the Digital Donut Shop per donut minted and held in wallet. Key holders will have access to the back office.

The First Vote

After minting concludes, 15% of primary sales will be donated to local food banks in 10 Cities/Towns voted on by Digital Donut holders.
Moving forward 15% of all secondary sales will be donated to 5 Cities/Towns voted on by the community monthly or bi monthly dependent on volume.

Community Events

Core artist events, including AMA’s, live streams and twitter spaces commence. First community art contest is announced, with Diamond Blender Development Badges distributed as part of community prizes, along with other prizes and Ethereum.

App Development

Digital Donut App Development begins alongside acquisition and development of the Digital Donut Shop in Decentraland.

Core is Core

Our first baking project with our core artist group will be free to claim for all holders.

Launch of Mobile App

The initial app will allow users to connect to Metamask to view their Digital Donuts NFT’s, access an interactive map of all donation locations, view upcoming events, discover community artists, keep up to date on Crypto/NFT news and more features to be announced.

Partnerships

Begin developing partnerships with national donut chains to bring Digital Donut NFT’s into new wallets and perks for Digital Donut holders

Physical Art Galleries

Hold and sponsor art galleries in real life as well as Decentraland, with special privileges and discounts, or exclusive access, depending on the event, given to attending Digital Donut Holders
