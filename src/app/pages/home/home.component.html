The world is currently experiencing a renaissance of art and technology, and Digital Donuts aims to be a central gathering place for those building and guiding this future. Digital Donut NFT’s are your access into this community.

Why The Digital Donut Shop?

In 3D art, the donut is often the first piece an artist will make. The first step of a great journey, sparking that realization of what is and can be possible. The same inspiration is happening in NFT’s, as digital architects build this future into Web 3.0. The donut is also a universally loved snack, the type you buy a dozen of and share with family and friends as you gather and discuss ideas. Our shop is a place for 3D artists of all skill levels to connect with a passionate community, whom in turn will be able to gather and collect great pieces from those just starting to shape the next era of art.

Roadmap

Opening Up Shop

Website and Discord launch, core initial group of core artists is finalized and whitelisting for presale begins

The First Batch

Original Batch of Digital Donuts are Minted via website

Key is Key

All wallets that minted will be given one week to mint a free(1) “Key to the Digital Donut Shop” per donut minted and held in wallet

The First Vote

One week after minting concludes, all Digital Donut holders will vote on the first group of 10 Cities/Towns for local food bank donations.
15% of primary sales will be donated to this list

Community Events

Sponsored 3D Artist live streams commence, 3D and 2D art contests are announced, with Diamond Blender Development Badges distributed as part of community prizes, along with other prizes and Ethereum

App Development

Digital Donut App Development begins alongside acquisition and development of the Digital Donut Shop in Decentraland

Custom First Editions

Access to mint one “First Edition Custom Digital Donut” per Original Digital Donut held, plus one per each burned, “Digital Donut Shop Key” for free(1).
Wallets with over 12 Digital Donuts plus a shop key will be able to mint one “First Edition Custom Digital Donut Box” for free

Launch of Mobile App

The initial app will allow users to connect to Metamask to view their Digital Donuts & Boxes, access an interactive map of all donation locations, view upcoming events, discover artists as well as their projects (along with Bio’s and links to the core Digital Donut artists), and more

Partnerships

Begin developing partnerships with national donut chains to bring Digital Donut NFT’s into new wallets and provide free physical donut perks for all holders

Physical Art Galleries

Hold and sponsor both physical and digital Art Galleries in real life as well as Decentraland, with special privileges and discounts given to attending Digital Donut Holders
